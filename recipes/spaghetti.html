Spaghetti and Meatballs Recipe

Here is a simple homemade spaghetti and meatball recipe with a list of ingredients and easy steps to follow

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Frozen Meatballs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meatballs: Preheat your air fryer to 375°F (190°C). Lightly spray the air fryer basket with nonstick cooking spray or brush with a bit of oil. Place the meatballs in a single layer in the basket, ensuring they aren't touching. Air fry the meatballs for 10-12 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through to ensure even cooking. They should be golden brown and cooked through with an internal temperature of 160°F (70°C).
  2. Make the Sauce: While the meatballs are cooking, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté 1 finely chopped onion and 2 minced garlic cloves for 3-4 minutes until softened. Add a 24-ounce jar of marinara sauce to the skillet, stirring well. Let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes. Once the air-fried meatballs are ready, gently add them to the sauce and let them simmer for a few minutes to absorb the flavors.
  3. Shape the dough: Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 2 portions. Roll out one portion on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Cook the Spaghetti: Meanwhile, cook 12 ounces of spaghetti in salted boiling water according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the spaghetti and serve it topped with the air-fried meatballs and sauce. Sprinkle with extra Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired, then enjoy!
